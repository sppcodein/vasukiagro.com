Our Story

VASUKI AGRO CHEMICALS LLP was founded in 2010 with a simple vision: to revolutionize farming by creating plant growth promoters using bio materials that are both effective and environmentally friendly.

Our journey began when a team of agricultural scientists and farmers came together with a shared concern about the excessive use of chemical fertilizers and pesticides in modern farming. They saw the toll these chemicals were taking on soil health, water quality, and biodiversity.

After years of research and development, we created our first breakthrough product - a bio-based plant growth promoter that significantly increased crop yields without the harmful side effects of chemical alternatives.

Today, VASUKI AGRO CHEMICALS LLP has grown into a trusted name in sustainable agriculture, serving farmers across India with our range of innovative, eco-friendly products.
