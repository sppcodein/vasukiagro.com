Our Story

Vasuki Agro Chemicals LLP, a pioneering agricultural company established on February 5th, 2024. We're dedicated to fostering sustainable farming practices by providing efficient crop solutions that significantly reduce agriculture's carbon footprint. We're proud to be a certified startup, recognized by both the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), Government of India, and K-tech, Government of Karnataka, underscoring our commitment to innovation and national development.

Our journey began with a strong belief in the power of research and development to transform agriculture. We've had the privilege of being incubated at two esteemed centers, enabling us to bring groundbreaking solutions to the market:

Atal Incubation Center - Nitte, NMAMIT Campus, Nitte, Karkala: Here, we successfully developed a unique Liquid Fermented Organic Manure derived from the biowaste slurry of Goshallas. This innovative product not only provides essential nutrients to crops but also addresses vital waste management challenges, promoting a circular economy in agriculture.

Centre for Cellular and Molecular Platforms (C-CAMP), GKVK, Bangalore: Our incubation at C-CAMP led to the development of a Liquid Micronutrient Mixture Fertilizer and Biostimulants inspired by the ancient wisdom of Vrikshayurveda's Kunapajala. These products are designed to enhance plant health, improve nutrient uptake, and boost overall crop productivity in an environmentally conscious manner.

Our company is pleased to announce it has entered into a Memorandum of Agreement (MOA) with the University of Agricultural Sciences, Raichur, for comprehensive quality control testing of our products. This strategic partnership underscores our unwavering commitment to maintaining the highest standards set forth by the Fertilizer Control Order (FCO). Through this collaboration, all our products will undergo rigorous testing at the university's state-of-the-art facilities, ensuring they consistently meet the stringent quality specifications required to benefit our customers and uphold regulatory compliance

We're also proud to hold the necessary licenses for manufacturing micronutrient mixture fertilizers, biostimulants, and liquid fermented organic manure as per the Fertilizer Control Order. This ensures that our products meet the highest standards of quality and regulatory compliance.

At Vasuki Agro Chemicals LLP, we're driven by a passion for creating a greener, more sustainable future for agriculture. Through continuous research and development, we strive to offer farmers effective, eco-friendly solutions that not only increase yields but also protect our planet for generations to come. Our Mission & Values

At VASUKI AGRO CHEMICALS LLP, we're driven by a set of core values that guide everything we do

Our Vision

To cultivate a future where every farm thrives sustainably, empowered by the synergy of ancient Indian wisdom of Agriculture - vrikshayurveda and modern agricultural science, yielding abundant and healthy harvests while enriching the soil and safeguarding our planet for generations to come.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be the farmer's most trusted partner in achieving superior crop health and maximizing yield potential through meticulously tailored nutrient solutions. We are committed to:

Empowering Farmers

Providing innovative and accessible fertilizer products that are farmer-centric, easy to apply, and demonstrably improve crop quality and quantity, ensuring their prosperity and food security.

Restoring Soil Vitality & Crop Health: Championing integrated nutrient management by blending the profound knowledge of ancient Indian Vrikshayurveda with cutting-edge agrochemicals, creating balanced and holistic solutions that enhance soil fertility, microbial activity, and overall crop resilience.

Driving Sustainable Agriculture: Leading the charge towards a greener agricultural landscape by actively reducing the reliance on conventional chemical fertilizers, thereby decreasing the agricultural carbon footprint and promoting environmentally responsible farming practices.

Delivering Tailored Solutions: Continuously innovating to develop a diverse range of products that precisely meet the unique nutritional demands of various soil types and crops, ensuring optimal nutrient uptake and minimizing waste.
